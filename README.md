# Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive **Excel dashboard** designed to analyze and visualize coffee shop sales data.  
It provides clear insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends using simple and effective visuals.

The goal of this project is to turn raw data into meaningful insights that support better business decisions.

---

## Business Problem
Coffee shops handle large amounts of daily transactions, but without proper analysis, it is difficult to:

- Identify peak sales hours  
- Understand customer purchasing patterns  
- Track best-performing products  
- Compare performance across locations  

This dashboard solves these problems by presenting the data in a clear and interactive way.

## Dashboard Features

### Sales by Hour
- Identifies peak business hours  
- Helps in staff and inventory planning  

### Product Category Analysis
- Compares categories like Coffee, Tea, Bakery, etc.  
- Highlights top revenue-generating categories  

### Weekly Order Trends
- Shows order distribution across days  
- Helps identify busiest and slowest days  

### Monthly Sales Trend
- Tracks growth from January to June  
- Useful for performance monitoring  

### Top 5 Products
- Highlights best-selling items  
- Helps in product focus and promotions  

### Location-Based Performance
- Compares sales across:
  - Astoria  
  - Hell’s Kitchen  
  - Lower Manhattan  

---

## Interactivity
The dashboard includes filters for:
- Month selection  
- Day selection  

This allows users to explore data dynamically and gain deeper insights.

---

## Key Insights
- Coffee is the highest revenue-generating category  
- Sales peak during morning hours  
- Friday is the busiest day of the week  
- Sales show consistent growth over months  
- Certain products dominate overall sales
